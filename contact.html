ORDER AND PRE-ORDER STATUS

For questions related to your order, please reach out to order[at]dunkrecords[dot]com with your order number in the email subject line.

We ship orders once a week, so if your order is less than a week old, it’s probably not shipped yet! Don’t worry, you will soon receive your shipping confirmation and tracking code via email.

If your order contains pre-order items, please note that it will only be shipped once every item in the order is in stock. We kindly ask you to check the pre-order item page regarding its status and availability before emailing us.

We're committed to shipping all orders timely and take your satisfaction seriously.

If your order is more than a week old or for any other issues with your order, please email us with a brief description of the issue + any pictures that can help us address it.

Please allow us 3 to 5 business days to reply to your email before sending us a new one. We appreciate your patience!

Thank you for your understanding and support!
